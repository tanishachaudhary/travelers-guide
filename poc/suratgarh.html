Welcome to Suratgarh!

We have the best for you!!!
Suratgarh is a town in distt. Sri Ganganagar of Rajasthan. This place is known for various things such as:
Suratgarh Super Thermal Power Plant: which was Rajasthan's FIRST SUPER THERMAL PLANT
Suratgarh Central State Farm: which is Asia's Biggest and largest Agriculture farm.
Suratgarh Air Force Station: Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman is posted here.
Suratgarh Military Station: As Suratgarh is close to Pakistan Border, so these stations are here.
Suratgarh Akashwani Station: You can listen to this All India Radio at 927 MW.

So, enjoy your visit to Suratgarh and unlock many more specialities!!!
